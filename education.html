EDUCATION









I began my education in kindergarten and attended "SK Keratong 10" in Muadzam Shah, which is close to where I live. I received three As and two Bs for my primary school on the SPM exam. After that, I continued my high school career after being accepted into "Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Perantau Damai" in 2014. (SMKPD). I have taken part in a variety of extracurricular sports events at this school, including under-15 and under-18 football tournaments, handball competitions, athletics competitions, and sepak takraw competitions. I joined the school's youth cadets for clubs and organisations. As for the course stream I followed from fourth to fifth grade, it included subjects like geography, business, and computer science.








I received acceptance to the Information Management programme at UiTM Machang in Kelantan for 2020. I gained a lot of knowledge from this course about how to locate, arrange, and organise information in accordance with my needs. I also learned the fundamentals of using Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe In design, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop. In the course IMD 311, web development, I also learned a lot about coding. I have also learned how to create bibliographic descriptions for books, particularly library materials, from this course. I'm planning on finishing this course in March 2023. I believe that sums up my educational background.
